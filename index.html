Slide 4
Who is VoyenBus

VoyenBus is an Argentine travel-tech company founded in 2004, focused on digital commercialization and distribution for bus operators.

  • It is not presented as a core ERP by itself.
  • It acts as a commercialization layer that connects to existing operator systems.
  • Its positioning is strategically valuable because it is independent and not tied to a transport group.
  • That independence matters in a market where operator trust and neutrality are essential.

Appendix
3-minute spoken version

Argentina is the most logical next market for ClickBus after Brazil, and the timing is unusually attractive. The sector is being reshaped, operators need better digital commercialization tools, and the market is still under-digitized.

VoyenBus is valuable because it is not just a software company. It is a local market-entry platform. It already has operator-facing infrastructure, real commercial relationships, and an existing B2C presence. That means ClickBus can enter with substance from day one, rather than spending years building credibility company by company.

The strategic case is simple: VoyenBus provides access, local execution, and white-label infrastructure; ClickBus provides scale, product muscle, AI, growth know-how, and capital. Together, they can create a category-leading position in Argentina faster than either could alone.
